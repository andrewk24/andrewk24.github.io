TI-84 Series

The TI-84 Plus series is the most popular of calculators that Texas Instruments sells. Originally released in 2004 as a follow-up to the TI-83 Plus, it introduced MathPrint, a staple feature of even lower end TIs today, as well as 2.5x the performance of the 83 Plus before it. It also introduces a clock and comes pre-loaded with new Flash Apps. Confusingly, there is no TI-84 non-plus model.

Also in 2004, the TI-84 Plus Silver Editionwas introduced, giving 1.5MB of Flash and a new case that comes in a variety of colors.

TI still sells the TI-84, but not the Silver Edition. They followed this up with the TI-84 Plus C Silver Edition and TI-84 Plus CE.
