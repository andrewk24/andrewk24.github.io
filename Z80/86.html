TI-86

The TI-86 is the follow up to the TI-85, and serves as the end to TI's short-lived engineering subseries of Z80 calculators. It's backwards compatible with the 85, with some updates to the OS and a more TI-83 esque shell. Software improvements over the 85 include the ability to graph 99 equations at once, parametric graphing style, 80x80 matricies, and 1/2 variable statistics.
