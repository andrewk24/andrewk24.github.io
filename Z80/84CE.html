TI-84 Plus CE

With a much thinner redesign and over 3x the performance of the TI-84 Plus C Silver Edition, The TI-84 Plus CE was a much bigger success when it launched in 2015. It kept the high-res color screen and rechargable battery, but updated the rest of the internals to be up to snuff to make it an all around far more usable. It uses the somewhat Z80-compatible Zilog eZ80 in order to achieve this higher clock speed.

As a result of the performance and popularity of the CE, the community over at Cemetech have started something of a new programming revolution for these calculators like it's predecessors. There's many toolchains and languages you can use to make your own Assembly programs for the CE, even if TI no longer supports it.
