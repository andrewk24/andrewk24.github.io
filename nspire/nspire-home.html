The Texas Instruments Nspire Series

TI's "Nspire Series" includes graphing calculators under the Nspire lineup. (Shocking.) These calculators include ARM processors.
So far, in the Nspire lineup, we have:

  • TI-Nspire / CAS / Touchpad / Touchpad CAS
  • TI-Nspire CX / CX CAS
  • TI-Nspire CX II / CX II CAS
The Nspire is TI's premium model of calculator. Along with including 68k-like CAS abilities on the CAS models, Nspire advertises connectivity features to keep the classroom connected.
It aims to be more than just a calculator, but instead a general purpose S.T.E.M. tool. The UI being based around documents and lessons reflects this, as well as the many S.T.E.M. accessories available for the platform.
They also have a plethora of programming tools and tutorials, some official, most not. You can learn more about your model's capabilities by visiting its specific page.
